RapidQuest Adventures

At RapidQuest Adventures, we believe in the power of nature, teamwork, and unforgettable experiences. Our mission is to deliver safe, exciting, and sustainable whitewater rafting adventures to people of all ages and skill levels.

History

Founded in 2025 by Macdonald Prince Yingura, RapidQuest Adventures started as a small weekend rafting guide service. Over time, our team grew, our trips expanded, and our reputation for safety and fun became unmatched in Idaho’s river country. Today, we serve thousands of adventurers each year and continue to grow our legacy on the water.
