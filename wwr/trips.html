Trip Descriptions

Class I – II

Gentle Currents Float

Perfect for families, beginners, and anyone who wants to enjoy the beauty of the river without the intensity of heavy rapids. This half-day guided float covers 8 km of scenic waterway with calm sections and light riffles. All gear and a riverside snack are included.

  • Duration: 4 hours
  • Difficulty: Beginner
  • Min. Age: 6 years
  • Price: GH₵ 180 / person
Class III

Adventure Rapids Run

The most popular trip on our roster. This full-day experience takes you through a dynamic mix of calm stretches and exciting Class III rapids that will get your heart pumping without pushing into extreme territory. Ideal for those with a little paddling confidence or a willingness to learn fast. Lunch on the riverbank is included.

  • Duration: 8 hours
  • Difficulty: Intermediate
  • Min. Age: 12 years
  • Price: GH₵ 320 / person
Class IV+

Extreme Whitewater Challenge

For experienced paddlers who want the ultimate test. This two-day expedition tackles the most demanding section of the river, including three named Class IV rapids and one Class V drop for those brave enough to attempt it. Camping overnight on a private riverside site is part of the experience. All technical safety equipment and a certified lead guide are included throughout.

  • Duration: 2 days
  • Difficulty: Expert
  • Min. Age: 16 years
  • Price: GH₵ 750 / person
